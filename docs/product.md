# Strategic Vision
**Operating System for Biology: From Protein Therapeutics to Biomaterials.**

Bigo Biologics builds the design infrastructure for the next industrial revolution, where functional proteins are programmable and manufactured with high precision.

# Product Guide

## Overview
bigo.bio serves as the digital front door for Bigo Biologics, a leader in AI-driven protein design automation. The platform highlights our core technology, the BDA+ (Biologics Design Automation) platform, and showcases our ability to design functional proteins, therapeutics, vaccine particles, and industrial enzymes.

## Core Platform: BDA+
The BDA+ platform treats amino acid sequences as code, utilizing AI foundation models and structural evaluation (AF3-like) coupled with a wet-lab feedback loop.
- **R&D Efficiency:** Reduces R&D cycles from ~3 years to 8 months.
- **Cost Reduction:** 60-70% lower R&D costs through reduced trial-and-error.
- **Cycle:** Design -> Build -> Test -> Learn -> Scale.

## Target Audience
- **Pharma & Biotech:** Teams seeking high-affinity binders and early drug discovery packages.
- **Vaccine Developers:** Organizations needing antigen display and nanocage architecture.
- **Industrial Clients:** Companies looking for optimized enzymes (activity, stability, specificity) for green manufacturing.

## Key Features & Requirements
- **BDA+ Showcase:** Detailed sections explaining the Biologics Design Automation workflow.
- **3D Molecule Display:** Core architectural support for rendering protein structures (e.g., binders, nanocages) directly in the browser.
- **Online IDE Gateway:** Entrance to the protein design studio for professional users.
- **Project/Product Showcase:** Highlighting success stories like the de novo PD-1 binder and Nature Materials published protein crystals.
- **Multilingual Support:** Full English and Simplified Chinese localization.
- **Blog Integration:** Connection to the technical blog at `blog.bigo.bio`.

## Application Paths
1. **Early Drug Discovery:** Standard packages for binder candidates and structural insights.
2. **Vaccine & Functional Display:** PoC for antigen display design and multimeric conformations.
3. **Industrial Enzymes & Materials:** PoC for enzyme activity and structural materials optimization.

## Design Identity (High-Level)
- **Style:** "Swiss Red" design—professional, clean, high-precision tech aesthetic.
- **Color Palette:** Black, white, or greyscale base with "Swiss Red" (#E62117) as the primary highlight color.
- **Layout:** Grid system-based, sharp right angles (no border radius), thin black outlines, and a blurred fixed floating navigation bar.

## Future Goals & Expansion

- **Platform Licensing:** Transitioning from project delivery to annual platform access and usage-based licensing.
- **3D Visualization:** Advanced interactive protein rendering using Three.js or similar technologies.
